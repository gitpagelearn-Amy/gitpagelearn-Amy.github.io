Born to resist the temptation of cheesecake

Main point: Willpower is a kind of physiological instinct, which is like pressure, protects us from self harm through continuous evolution. When the brain and body work at the same time ,we can overcome impulse.

Stress response the instinct to fight or run for life inherited from one’s ancestors

Symptom : rapid heartbeat, chin fight, high mental tension

Information enters the amygdala (alarm system) through the eyes. The amygdala uses the advantage of being in the middle of the brain to quickly transmit information: the adrenal gland releases pressure hormones, the energy stored in the form of fat and sugar enters the blood vessels and liver, which is then transported to the muscles and lungs inhales enough air.

Willpower choose to do something more important and dealing with the desire that cannot be eliminated

Deal with the inner impulse of the body. Instead of speeding up as stress response , the prefrontal cortex should slow down the brain regions that control heart rate, blood pressure and respiration.

Heart rate variability Physical willpower “storage”

Heart rate variability reflects the degree of willpower. For example, people who abstain from alcohol may continue to be sober if they see an increase in the variability of heart rate. In contrate, if the variability of heart rate decreases, they may be impulsive enough to drink again.

Training:

Eat more unprocessed food , food made of plants and less junk food.

Live in a place with good air quality.

Reduce the rate of respiratory (4-6 times per minute).

Keep doing sports which you want to do.

Get enough sleep.

Relax.
