Self-introduction

My Chinese name is Zhao Jie and my English name is Amy. I am from the tenth grade of the international Department of Jinling High School.

My family thinks I am an introverted girl but they do not worry about me. However, they also advise me to be more confident so that I can get more chances and be more energetic in activities. As a result, I will gain more experience. My friends generally think I am very helpful and patient and one reason for this is that I am happy to repeat explaining exercises until they understand. Moreover,they also said I am generous because I never mind lending my books and stationery to them. Furthermore,people in our community consider me as a polite but shy girl as a result of that I am not very active in the activities which usually taken part in by a lot of people.

One of my proudest achievements is that I can play guzheng and trombone well. I kept practising guzheng almost every day until I got to the certificate of highest level for amateur scholars. For this reason,I was chosen as a member of the school’s guzheng team from primary school to middle school and I have taken part in many shows and competitions during that period. Now I play the trombone in the orchestra of my high school which has taught me how to work well with others as well as do everything with a sense of responsibility.
