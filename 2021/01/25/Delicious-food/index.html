Delicious food

British people generally prefer cooking methods: stewing, barbecue and frying. They have unique ways of cooking. They are also particular about breakfast which has a great variety. Afternoon tea is from the UK. Dinner is the most important part of daily life for British people. They usually choose a late meal time , drinking and chatting to promote the friendships among diners. A dinner may take several hours.

Roast beef

When you order this dish, the waiter will ask you whether you like it raw or cooked. Yorkshire pudding is also famous as a side dish.

Yorkshire pudding

Yorkshire pudding is similar to soft bread in taste. It is slightly salty, in the shape of a coffee cup. It is easy to absorb gravy so it is eaten with roast beef.

Fried fish and chips

It is a cheap and convenient food. It can be eaten with salt or vinegar (many British people use both) .

Victorian Style afternoon tea

There is a famous style of afternoon tea- Victorian style,which including many kinds of muffins, tartes and sandwiches.

Victoria sponge cake

Victoria sponge cake is a kind of cakes for British afternoon tea. It is made by one layer of cream and one layer of jam.

Bitter beer

Bitter beer is the most famous beer in Britain. At first, it is an alternative to traditional malt liquor and has low alcohol content.

Easton cake

A special kind of desserts which is made from strawberries, cream and pastry.
