The first competition of my life

发表于 2021-08-31

An event which sparked a period of personal growth and a new understanding of myself was my first participation in the musical instrument competition held by my primary school.

I used to be a shy girl. Although I enjoyed playing a kind of traditional chinese musical instrument called the guzheng, I was not brave enough to play it in front of the public. As a result, almost no one knew that I could play the guzheng apart from my relatives and teachers.

However, this situation changed when I was in the third grade. My music teacher thought that I should be willing to take challenges to show everyone my unique skills and be more confident, so she recommended me to the school to take part in the instrument competition. I wanted to refuse, but my parents also encouraged me to take this rare opportunity. Finally, I made up my mind to prepare for this competition seriously as I did not want to let down the people in my life who have always supported me. During the preparation for this competition, I was still very nervous and anxious. However, my parents told me that the meaning and purpose of the competition was not only to win prizes, but also to help me become a better person through this kind of experience. From then on, I started to enjoy practicing playing the guzheng and felt happy because both my ability and my confidence improved. After several months of hard preparation, I won the second prize. I found I was able to achieve the success which I had wanted.

Through my experience of this event, I changed from being timid and introverted to being brave, confident and able to cherish any opportunities in my life. The music I played at the ten-year celebration of junior school was appreciated by my classmates and teachers. I am now a member of the high school symphonic band. I also take an active part in other club activities or subject competitions. I really enjoy the feeling of self-improvement.
