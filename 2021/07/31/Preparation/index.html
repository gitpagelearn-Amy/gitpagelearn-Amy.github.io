发表于 2021-07-31

1.Choose one aspect of gender inequality, and list more than TWO evidences to back up your statement.

There are gender disparity in political empowerment.

Evidence 1:Women in national parliaments are also severely underrepresented. Till April 2021, of 46204 parliaments seats across the world, only 25.5% (11770) of all national parliamentarians are women. The website where you find this: http://data.ipu.org/women-ranking?month=4&year=2021

Evidence 2:Some reports say that very few women have served as head of state in the highest political position. Only 22 countries have had women leaders as the head of state or government, and there has never been a woman in the highest position in other 119 countries. The website where you find this: https://www.unwomen.org/en/about-us/ about-un-women.

2.Find one treaty/ protocol/ declaration/ collective action plan signed based on UN resolution or regional consensus. State your opinion to it, like advantage/ disadvantage, historical milestone, leading to some act or so.

The United Nations General Assembly passed resolution on COVID-19. The resolution was passed by 169 out of 193 countries. Only the United States and Israel objected it. Member states strengthen international cooperation, solidarity and mutual assistance to fight against COVID-19. The resolution stated that international cooperation, multilateralism, solidarity and mutual assistance are the only ways for the world to effectively respond to global crises such as COVID-19. It called on all countries to treat everyone equally. The resolution supported the global ceasefire initiative proposed by the UN Secretary-General, paid close attention to the impact of the epidemic on areas of armed conflict, and continued to support UN peacekeeping operations. The resolution urged member states, political leaders and religious leaders to not only promote tolerance and unity but also oppose violence and discrimination. It also urged member states to strengthen health systems and social care systems. Moreover, it urged member states and other stakeholders to deal with the direct social and economic impact of COVID-19. This resolution cracked down on unilateralism, protectionism and hegemonism. At the same time, it promoted international cooperation and encouraged the practice of multilateralism. Many countries cooperated to effectively control the epidemic. For example, Russia assisted Wuhan with personal protection and medical supplies many times. Pakistan, with its underdeveloped economy and low level of medical care, also solicited medical supplies from public hospitals across the whole country to provide assistance to our country, which was very touching. Our country has also donated supplies to many countries and sent teams of medical experts.

3.Choose ONE delegation you prefer and state reasons. This answer will be the critical reference to delegation arrangement.

My favourite United Nations delegation is the Chinese delegation. Because the Chinese delegation can always put forward creative initiatives that are beneficial to the world according to current events. For example, the thought of building a community with a shared future for mankind was supported by most countries.
